HOW IT WORKS

[ Step 1 — Scan ]
Point & Capture

Aim your camera at any real-world text — signs, menus, books, messages. Advanced OCR reads it instantly. No typing, no copying, no "I'll look it up later."

[ Step 2 — Learn ]
Wordscope it

Advanced AI generates a complete flashcard in seconds — translation, reading (furigana, pinyin, romanization), and grammar context tuned for CJK scripts and exam vocab.

[ Step 3 — Review ]
Spaced Repeat

Leitner-based spaced repetition brings the right cards back at the right time. Your flashcards are always front and center — ready whenever your day gives you a moment.
